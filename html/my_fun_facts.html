My Favorite Book

The Spanish Love Deception - Elena Armas

“I’ll be your date to the wedding.”

A TikTok sensation, this rom-com about a young woman who agrees to fake date a colleague and bring him to her sister’s wedding has “everything you could want in a romance”.

You can purchase this book at Amazon, if you delight yourself in a good read!

My Favorite Song

Thank God - Kane Brown and Katelyn Brown

"Thank God" is one of my favorite songs from all time. It is a song from Kane Brown dedicated to his wife as he sings about how he thanks God everyday for sending him, her. It is a cute, romantic song.

You can listen to the full version of this song at Spotify!
